What is OS X Setups?

OS X Setups was once founded to share awesome workspace setups on Instagram.
Inspired by the many beautiful workspaces from the Instagram community it was time to
create a page and put all these setups in one place.

Get Inspired

The main focus is layed on inspiring the Instagram community on how to create a setup they love to work with. This usually happens through reposting awesome workspaces at the Instagram page. It's important to us that the original creator is always linked and credit is given.

Get inspired

Advertise with us

As many people know there are many sponsored posts on Instagram these days. This makes it possible for companies to build their brand on this platform. We offer this as long as we agree with the brand/ the product. That means we will not promote trash or something that has nothing to do with this page. If you are interested in a deal, feel free to contact osxsetups@gmail.com. Basically we can offer you 2 ways to promote a product or your brand.

Giveaways

4 giveaways until now which all went great. The manufacturers offer a certain product that will be gifted to the winner of a giveaway. Usually a giveaway goes on one week and the conditions of participation are liking and commenting the giveaway post which shows your product in an aesthetic environment.

After the winner is determined by an application made for Instagram giveaways, we are going to contact the winner via Direct Message and look for his address. This address will be forwarded to the manufacturer so he can send out the product to the lucky winner.

Sponsored Posts

Sponsored Posts are a good way to promote a certain product. It's important that we will not post plain product pictures or something like this. What we need is a workspace including your product. So this way of promoting a product is product placement.

First you have to contact us via email and make sure to give us enough information about your product and the post. After we understood what you want to promote, we gonna offer you a certain price which totally depends on the product you are promoting.
